Vada Pav

Description

Vada Pav is Mumbai's iconic, affordable street food: a spicy, deep-fried potato fritter (vada) made from mashed potatoes and spices, coated in chickpea flour batter, served inside a soft bread bun (pav) with tangy tamarind, spicy green, and dry garlic chutneys, offering a flavorful, handheld snack.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, Onion, Chilli, Gunder, Garlic, Mustard Seeds
  • Curry leaves, Turmeric poweder, salt, lemon juice, coriander
  • Gram flour, water, turmeric, salt
  • Oil, Pav, green chutney, garlic chutney, fried chilli

Steps to make a vada pav

  1. Boil and mash potatoes.
  2. Heat oil; add mustard seeds, curry leaves, ginger, garlic, green chilli.
  3. Mix this into potatoes with turmeric, salt, lemon, coriander.
  4. Make round balls of the mixture.
  5. Prepare besan batter, dip the balls in it.
  6. Deep-fry till golden.
  7. Slice pav, add chutneys, place the vada inside.
  8. Serve hot
