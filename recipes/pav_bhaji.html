Pav Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food featuring a spicy, thick mashed vegetable curry (bhaji) served with butter-toasted bread rolls (pav), characterized by its rich flavor from a special spice blend (pav bhaji masala), generous butter, and tangy garnishes of fresh onions and lemon, originating as a quick meal for mill workers in Mumbai.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cauliflower
  • Green peas
  • Capsicum (bell pepper)
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter, Oil, Ginger-garlic paste, chilli, lemon. coriander
  • Pav (bread rolls)

Steps to make a Dosa:

  1. Boil and mash potatoes, cauliflower, peas.
  2. Heat butter and oil; saut√© onion, ginger-garlic, green chilli.
  3. Add tomatoes, capsicum, cook till soft.
  4. Add pav bhaji masala, chilli powder, salt.
  5. Mix in mashed vegetables and mash well.
  6. Add water, simmer till thick and flavorful.
  7. Toast pav with butter.
  8. Serve hot with butter, onion, lemon, coriander.
